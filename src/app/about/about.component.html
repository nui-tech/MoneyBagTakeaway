OUR STORY

At Moneybag Thai Takeaway, we are passionate about quality food. You will find our menu refreshing. We are not overly concerned with all the latest fashions in food because we know you still love your old favourites.

Remember the days when there was a 'Take away food shop' on practically every corner? Now you'll find delis, patisseries, designer restaurants or worse... a drive through restaurant in their place; we're not knocking them, all we are saying is that you can't beat a good old fashioned take away food shop.
When it comes to a great quality, quick and affordable meals, you can rely on us.

OUR PROMISE
